Fintech Platform PM B2B

Payment platform product ownership at BillDesk

Technical Product Owner · BillDesk · India

The problem

A major client product lacked a dedicated owner. Engineering, business, and client teams were misaligned — leading to delays, rework, and frustrated stakeholders.

My approach

Took end-to-end ownership of the product. Built documentation from scratch, ran sprint ceremonies, created a stakeholder communication cadence, and became the single source of truth.

The outcome

Delivered a complex backend migration on time with full stakeholder alignment. Received director-level recognition for translating technical complexity into business clarity.

What I learned

The most valuable thing a PM can bring to a technical project is not technical knowledge — it's the ability to make everyone in the room feel heard, informed, and aligned. That's the work nobody writes a ticket for, but everybody notices when it's missing.

How I work

My PM approach

Clarity before velocity

I'd rather spend an extra day getting everyone aligned than sprint for a week in the wrong direction. Ambiguity is expensive. Clarity compounds.

Document everything that matters

Good documentation isn't bureaucracy — it's respect for your team's time. If it's not written down, it doesn't exist.

Every audience deserves the right language

Engineers need precision. Executives need context. Clients need confidence. I speak all three — and I know when to switch.

Ship, learn, iterate

Perfect is the enemy of shipped. I push for momentum, create feedback loops, and treat every release as a question, not an answer.
